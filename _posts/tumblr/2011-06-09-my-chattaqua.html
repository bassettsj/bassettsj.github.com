Chattaqua Park was my place of refuge while I was in school at the University of Colorado at Boulder. It was a few minutes away from campus, but the feeling I got there was worlds apart. In our Sustainable Design siminar, we were asked to create a project about a natural environment that we felt a sense of place with. I chose to create a video on Chattaqua Park.
