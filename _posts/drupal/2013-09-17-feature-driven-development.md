* Context
* Taxonomies
* Rules
* Menu definitions
* Content type definitions and field defitions
* Search Servers
* Search Indexes
* Configuration for Contributed Modules
* Image Styles
* Text Filters
* CKEditor Settings
* Rules and Rules Sets
